Why Threnty?

👁

20-20-20 Rule

Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 meters away for 20 seconds. Threnty keeps you on schedule, automatically.

📍

Menu Bar Timer

Lives quietly in your menu bar with a live countdown. No Dock icon, no extra windows — just the info you need.

🖥

Full-Screen Overlay

When it's time to rest, a gentle overlay appears on every display with a 20-second countdown, then dismisses itself.

🔁

Set & Forget

Hit Start and walk away. Threnty cycles automatically — work, break, repeat — until you choose to stop.
