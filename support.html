Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start the timer?

Click the Threnty icon in your menu bar to open the popover, then press Start. The countdown will appear in the menu bar and a break reminder will trigger after 20 minutes.

How do I stop or skip a break?

To skip an active break, press Skip on the full-screen overlay. To stop the timer entirely, open the menu bar popover and press Stop.

Can I change the 20-minute interval?

The current version uses fixed 20-minute work and 20-second break durations as prescribed by the 20-20-20 rule. Configurable intervals may be added in a future release.

Where is my data stored?

Any preferences are stored locally on your Mac. Nothing is sent to any server. If you delete the app, your preferences will be removed.

Does Threnty run at login?

Not automatically. You can enable "Open at Login" in System Settings → General → Login Items after installing the app.
