Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Overview

Threnty is designed with your privacy as a core principle. The app collects no personal data whatsoever. Your preferences stay on your device and are never transmitted anywhere.

Data Collection

Threnty does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account creation or login required
  • No personal identifiers collected (name, email, etc.)
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No cookies or similar technologies
  • No advertising or ad-related tracking

Data Storage

Any app preferences (such as timer settings) are stored locally on your Mac using standard macOS storage mechanisms. This data is:

  • Stored only on your device
  • Not synced to any cloud service
  • Not accessible to the developer or any third party
  • Removed when you delete the app

Third-Party Services

Threnty uses no third-party SDKs, analytics services, or tracking tools. There are no integrations that send data to external servers.

Children's Privacy

Since Threnty collects no data from any user, it is safe for use by anyone. No information is collected from users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Since Threnty collects no data, significant changes are unlikely.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our Support page.
